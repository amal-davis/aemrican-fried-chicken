Our Story

American Fried Chicken began as a tiny family counter with a bold idea: fried chicken should be simple, honest, and unforgettable. We travelled, learned, and tested until the coating snapped with a signature crunch—then we built a team to share it with the world.

  • Hand-marinated chicken with a guarded blend of American spices.
  • Cooked fresh in small batches—never parked under heat lamps.
  • Local partnerships for consistent quality and traceability.
  • Community-first culture: we hire, train, and grow from within.
